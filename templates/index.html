About Us

Dixit Health Care (Alternative Therapy Treatment Center)

Welcome to Dixit Health Care, your trusted destination for holistic healing through natural therapies. Located in Shyam Marg Near Vichitra Veer Hanuman Mandir, Shujalpur Mandi, Dist. Shajapur (M.P.), we are dedicated to offering alternative and Ayurvedic treatments that restore balance and wellness.

Our expert team of practitioners, led by Dr. Vijay Shyam Dixit (CHW, BAMS (AM), BPT, BEMS, Chiropractic MD, Reg. No.: 22561) and Dr. Pooja Dixit (B.Sc., BEMS, D. Chiro - Specialist in Women's Diseases, Reg. No.: 20769), bring extensive knowledge and experience in holistic healthcare. We integrate Ayurveda, Chiropractic techniques, and other natural therapies to help our patients achieve optimal health.

Our center offers specialized treatments such as Ayurvedic Panchkarma, Hijama (Cupping Therapy), Ayurveda Consultation, Acupressure, Vastu Consultation, Naturopathy, Foot Massage Therapy, and Chiropractic Therapy (Bone Setting). Each treatment is designed to promote natural healing, relieve pain, and improve overall well-being.

At Dixit Health Care, we believe in a patient-centric approach, focusing on the root cause of ailments rather than just symptoms. Our goal is to empower individuals with holistic healing techniques, guiding them toward a healthier and more balanced life.
